# HaakonsVikinger
Norway (Northern Fronts) WW2 Antistasi

Haakon's Vikinger (or Haakon's Vikings) is a modification of Antistasi, namely the Polish WW2 Antistasi scenario.

1944, it's been almost four years since the King Haakon and his Government was forced to flee Norway. With horrible events in recent memory, like the Telav√•g tradegy, the population of Svartmarka has been getting restless. Remnants of the Norwegian army and civilians who were tired with the Nazi occupation have been operating in Milorg since the start of the war, but with Germany's recent major setbacks, it's time to do more than disrupt and sabotage; it's time to deal a significant blow to the Reich.

Through Milorg's association with the Government in exile, over in London, Haakon's Vikinger: a group of the Norwegian Army local to Svartmarka has managed to make contact with a section of British Army commandos as well as other foreign volunteers, who have provided some supplies to compliment the weapons and uniforms left over from the Norwegian Army.

---------------

If you don't want to fight the Soviets, you can change the gamemode in parameters. Fighting the Soviets is still set as the default because it sounds more fun.
