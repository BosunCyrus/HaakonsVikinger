# HaakonsVikinger
Norway (Northern Fronts) WW2 Antistasi

Haakon's Vikinger (or Haakon's Vikings) is a modification of Antistasi, namely the Polish WW2 Antistasi scenario.

1944, it's been almost four years since King Haakon and his Government was forced to flee Norway. With horrible events in recent memory, like the Telav√•g tradegy, the population of Svartmarka has been getting restless. Remnants of the Norwegian army and civilians who were tired with the Nazi occupation have been operating in Milorg since the start of the war, but with Germany's recent major setbacks, it's time to do more than disrupt and sabotage; it's time to deal a significant blow to the Reich.

Through Milorg's association with the Government in exile, over in London, Haakon's Vikinger: a group of the old Norwegian Army local to Svartmarka has managed to make contact with allied forces and British Army commandos. These forces have promised to provide supplies and intelligence to supplement Haakon's Vikinger's dwindling supply of Norwegian weapons and uniforms.

Their first tip? A stockpile of rifles that were taken from surrendered Norwegian forces are being held nearby in the town of Lakselv. Gather up whatever rifle or shotgun your grandfather has hung up on the wall and prepare to move!
